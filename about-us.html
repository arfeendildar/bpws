ABOUT US

BMC Patient Welfare Society is the name of a dream to help patients at BMC gain access to basic health facilities in a troublefree manner. Its an effort to debunk and decode complicated hospital related procedures for our underprivileged class.
Objectives:
Following are the aims and objectives of BPWS:
* To play role in capacity building of society
* To raise awareness amongst the community about various infectious diseases.
* To provide mental and psychological aid to patients.
* To provide financial aid to underprivaleged patients
* To raise funds for helping patients and improving hospital facilities at BMC.
* To conduct surveys regarding public health problems.
* To provide counseling to patients and attendents.
Goals:
To ensure not only a healthy and aware community that is proactive regarding medical procedures but also to erase the plight of discrimination. And maintaining a positive ‘’ hospital to patient ‘’ behaviour In shaa Allah!
With Allah’s help,your generous aid and support BPWS embarks this journey of service( Fee Sabilillah)
